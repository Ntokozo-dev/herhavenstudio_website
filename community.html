Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a class?

+

Visit our Classes page, select your preferred class, and click "Book a class". Fill in your details and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

+

You can cancel up to 24 hours before your class. Contact us via the general enquiries form or email support@herhavenstudio.co.za.

Do I need experience for strength classes?

+

No! We offer classes for all levels, from complete beginners to advanced. Our trainers will guide you through modifications for your fitness level.

Are classes available online?

+

Yes, many of our classes are available both in-studio and online. When booking, you can choose your preferred format.

What's your refund policy?

+

Unused class credits can be refunded within 30 days of purchase. For more details, review our full policy on the About Us page.
